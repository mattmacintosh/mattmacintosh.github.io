Curated by Noa Bronstein and Katherine Dennis. A group of images and artefacts staged in the Gibson House Museum, Toronto. The images were produced from scanned photos of the Gibson family selected by the Museum’s curator. The artefacts were selected from the Collection at the Orillia Museum of Art & History. The artefacts titles are from Canadian Living’s Top 15 Home Staging Tips (Installation photos by Toni Hafkenscheid)
